<h3><strong>Overview:</strong></h3>
<p>We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Program/Project Manager to support our Government Projects team. The Government Projects team works closely with Product,&nbsp; Engineering and Customers to bring new and innovative ideas to life. This role requires a strong focus on project management, customer satisfaction, on time completion of projects, technical consulting, implementation, and support services to government customers. The ideal candidate should have a strong background in networking, computer security, professional services, project management, customer relationship management.</p>
