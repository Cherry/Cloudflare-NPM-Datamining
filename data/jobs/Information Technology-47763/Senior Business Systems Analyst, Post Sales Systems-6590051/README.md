<h4>What you'll do</h4>
<p>We are seeking an experienced Senior Business Systems Analyst to join our team, focusing on Salesforce Service Cloud and related post sales applications.&nbsp;</p>
<p>In this role, you will be closely working with our Customer Support and Customer Success business stakeholders to deliver a world class experience for both customers and our internal users. This individual will work closely with the IT product management team, Developers and QA team members.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions, driving process improvements and system optimizations across our Salesforce ecosystem.</p>
