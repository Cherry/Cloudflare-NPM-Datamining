<p><strong>Role Summary</strong></p>
<p>Security at Cloudflare is a diverse, focused team committed to helping secure both Cloudflare and our customers.&nbsp; In this role, you’ll work closely with a Senior Technical Program Manager and a team of cybersecurity experts to develop a plan and communicate progress toward delivering change.&nbsp; You’ll succeed in this role if you are able to work well in ambiguous situations, want to lead cross-functional teams to work together to make a big impact, and enjoy making the complex look simple. Must be able to clearly communicate, work in a team environment, and enjoy analysis work.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>About the Internship Program</strong></p>
<p>The ideal summer intern is passionate about making the Internet a better place. You will work with a strong team of talented professionals. Your work this summer will impact hundreds of millions of web surfers. We like to get things done, so we are looking for interns who are curious and can take on and complete projects. This is a great opportunity for those who want to learn the ins and outs of Cybersecurity.&nbsp;</p>
